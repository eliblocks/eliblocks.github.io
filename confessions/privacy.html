Privacy Policy

Confessions Discord Bot

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

What We Collect

When you use the Confessions bot, we store:

  • Your Discord user ID and username
  • The text of confessions you submit
  • The Discord server where you submitted them

We do not collect any information beyond what you directly provide through the bot.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to operate the bot — storing your confessions, sharing them with other users, and identifying your account. Your confession text is sent to Google Gemini for AI processing. Google's use of that data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Other users in your Discord server may see your confession — that's how the bot works. Beyond that and the third-party services described above, your data is not shared.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your confessions and account data are retained as long as the bot is active. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.
