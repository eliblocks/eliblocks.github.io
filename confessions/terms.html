What This Is

Confessions is a Discord bot that lets users share confessions and see what other people in their server have shared.

Your Content

You own whatever you write. By submitting a confession, you give us permission to store it, process it, and share it with other users in your Discord server.

Content Rules

Confessions are reviewed using AI (Google Gemini) before being accepted. Don't submit content that is:

  • Hateful, threatening, or harassing
  • Containing personal or identifying information about other people
  • Spam or nonsense

We can remove any content or restrict access at our discretion.
